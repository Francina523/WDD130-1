My Background

I have experienced various stages in my professional journey, and each of them has shaped my current work principles and beliefs. I have excelled in multiple project financial management and resource mobilization in the finance department, and I consider my greatest strength to be my capacity to adapt to any challenge that comes my way.

Who is Francina and what has she done?

Francina is a dedicated and driven individual who, in her mid-thirties, joined an organization focused on advocating for women in the finance department. Despite having only one grant to finance the program at the beginning, her passion and commitment to the cause have led to remarkable growth. Six years later, she is now managing eight grants in a year, showcasing her exceptional ability to expand and sustain the organization’s initiatives. Francina’s unwavering dedication and passion for seeing the growth and continuity of the organization’s mission have been instrumental in her success in managing multiple grants and driving positive change within the organization.
